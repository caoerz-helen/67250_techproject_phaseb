Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art.

Discover immersive exhibitions, digital art, and interactive spaces designed to spark curiosity.

At MonoMuse, visitors can explore contemporary works that combine design, code, media, and visual storytelling. Our exhibitions highlight how technology shapes culture and everyday life, while our programs encourage visitors to participate, experiment, and engage with art in new ways. From gallery installations to hands-on digital labs, MonoMuse is designed to feel welcoming, creative, and easy to navigate both in person and online.
